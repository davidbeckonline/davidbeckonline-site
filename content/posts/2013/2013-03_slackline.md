As I still feel a little bit weak due the flew which caught me after the trip to Italy last week, this was the perfect choice to spend the afternoon:

Mild temperatures, sunlight, ... spring is definitely arriving. I had some great days in the snow this season, so I can confidently say "Goodbye winter - welcome spring".
